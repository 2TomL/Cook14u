About Me

I'm a lifelong gamer, I started streaming on Twitch.tv/cook14u just to be able to watch my gameplay in hopes of improving... It turned into this growing community I am very much blessed to be a part of. Mostly on Warzone but I play some of mostly everything... There's some laughs, there's some pains, but mostly the channel revolves around self reflecting to improve.
